Welcome to Summer Camp!

Get ready to learn, play, create, and go places. We are very excited to introduce you to our weekly summer day camp programs. Our primary goal is to provide a safe and fun environment for all our participants. Together we unplug, play, swim, keep active, make friends, and expand our abilities; we become more confident, resilient, and independent!

Note: This page contains information on the day-to-day operations of camp. For safety precautions and COVID-19 information, please see our safety page. This page will be continually updated as we get guidance from the state and CDC.

Facilities

During camp hours, our staff will be responsible for keeping the following areas safe and secure. Campers are limited to designated areas and are not permitted in any space without their counselor. Campers who breach these boundaries will be disciplined according to our Rules and Disciplinary Policy. Areas included for our use are the playgrounds, MS Multipurpose Field, Holman Baseball Field, Capano Field, Moulton Baseball Field, HS Tennis Courts, Nature Land, inside and outside the Middle School / Pond Cove, the Community Center, and the pool.

Communication

Any additional information or changes in our daily schedule will be emailed to you and posted on our website at www.capecommunityservices.org. If you need to speak to your camper or have a change in your camper's daily schedule please call the appropriate phone number from the Contact Us page. Any and all other camp related questions, payments, cancellations, etc. may be directed to Community Services at 799-2868. Summer Hours are June 21 - August 27, 7:30 am - 3:30 pm.

Medications

A Medication Distribution form must be completed for any prescription and nonprescription medication(s) we need to administer to your child during camp hours. Medications should be clearly labeled in its original container, inside a ziplock bag, with the child's name on it, and left at the camp desk with a camp administrator. Click here for the form to authorize camp staff to dispense medication.

Emergency Procedures

Staff and counselors are CPR certified, equipped with walkie talkie and first aid kit to treat any minor accident. Parents will be contacted immediately if a doctor's attention is required. If immediate medical care is necessary, an ambulance will be called first and then the parents. If the parents are unable to accompany the child to the nearest emergency room, they will be accompanied by a staff person.

Late Fees

Pick-up from camp is promptly at 3:00 pm. Please call us if you know you are running more than 10 minutes late. After 3:15 pm a late fee will be charged, unless your child is registered for after care.
  • 15 minutes $25 late fee.
  • 16 - 30 minutes $30 late fee.
  • Over 31 minutes $40 late fee.
